## 1. The opportunity in one line

**The "AI copilot for math teachers" for the Western Balkans and Turkish/Albanian-speaking markets — starting from a defensible national monopoly in North Macedonia.**

## 2. Problem → Solution → Why now

- **Problem:** Teachers spend hours on curriculum-aligned tests, plans, and grading. Global tools (IXL, Quizlet, Kahoot) don't fit local curricula, languages, or exam formats.
- **Solution:** MisMath AI turns the national curriculum (MОН/БРО) into a structured knowledge base and layers AI generation, grading, extraction, and interactive labs on top — in MK/SQ/TR/EN, in under two minutes per artifact.
- **Why now:** (1) Generative AI makes per-teacher content generation economically feasible; (2) the product is **already live and production-grade** (23 Jul 2026 audit: real RAG, tamper-proof grading, billing, 3,081 tests, 0 TS errors); (3) the SQ/TR localizations already shipped open a regional path that single-language players can't quickly follow.

## 3. Market

| Layer | Definition | Size |
|---|---|---|
| **Beachhead (SOM)** | North Macedonia math teachers | ≈2,500 |
| **Serviceable (SAM)** | MK all-subject teachers + Albania/Kosovo/Turkey math teachers | ≈150,000+ **[ASSUMPTION]** |
| **Total (TAM)** | Regional teachers (Western Balkans + Turkey) + subject expansion | ≈300,000+ **[ASSUMPTION]** |

**Beachhead logic:** win a small, defensible market where curriculum knowledge is the barrier (≈2,500 teachers, ≈€40k/yr at a national license), then expand regionally on the already-built SQ/TR localizations and across subjects.

## 4. Business model

- **Pro Teacher:** €25/year (1,500 MKD) — unlimited AI generation.
- **School license:** €300–800/year per school (up to 20 teachers) **[ASSUMPTION]** — admin, analytics, curriculum editor.
- **National (MОН) license:** by contract — all teachers nationwide.
- **Unit economics:** infrastructure ≈ €0.10/active user/day; Pro margin positive below ~1 AI call / 1.5 min; free-tier credit cap limits exposure. **Domestic break-even ≈ 36 Pro users.**

## 7. Moat / defensibility

1. **Curriculum knowledge base** (grades 1–13 + vocational, 73 real state exams, БРО standards mapping) — costly to replicate, the barrier global players lack.
2. **Localization** (MK/SQ/TR/EN) — regional reach competitors don't have.
3. **AI engineering depth** (real RAG, tamper-proof server-side grading, extraction pipeline, model-agnostic tiering) — beyond what local competitors can build.
4. **Data flywheel (emerging):** community scenario bank + telemetry improve content quality with usage.
